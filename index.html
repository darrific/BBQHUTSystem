About Us


Our Mission

We have a simple goal: to make the best barbeque that we can, in our own way, and get our barbecue to the most people that we can. A combination of intense flavors, stemming from our use of our variety special sauces (including our trademark Barbecue Sauce), is our signature style, and we want to bring this signature style to people all over Trinidad.






Our Story

1997. San Fernando, Trinidad and Tobago. “Chicken and Chips” was fast becoming the predominant ‘Fast Food’ in Trinidad and Tobago. Mr. David Bukhal recognized that a lot of newly opened Fast Food Eateries did not serve foods that were Halal, ignoring the needs of the Muslim Community of Trinidad. Having worked in various eateries during the 80s, he was familiar with the various procedures involved in operating such a venue. After some consideration, he and his brothers Kamal (A.K.A KB), Sohan Alan (A.K.A The Kernel) and Sasenarine (A.K.A Popee), decided to undertake a pioneering venture, the result of which was the creation of Original BBQ Hut, a Fast Food Restaurant where Halal foods were served, which could be enjoyed by both Muslims and non-Muslims alike.
The original location was on South Trunk Road, Bel Air, La Romaine, at a simple venue consisting of two tents and five tables. We soon began to develop and subsequently offer our now-signature line of sauces, the recipes of which are still kept secret to this day. Following this, they experienced phenomenal growth, surpassing all expectations. Due to this and other changes occurring within the Business, the venue was relocated to Cipero Road, Victoria Village, San Fernando. Our organization of our workers created an excellent flow, enabling orders to be filled in mere minutes, if not less.
BBQ Hut currently resides on 3 acres of prime real estate, with a staff of over 50 workers. The Bukhal Brothers are now aided by their children (Abdul-Qadir, Kamron, Cashmirr, Khadijah, Jaleela, Saleena, Aleem, Aleema, Alaana, and Ahmad) in the running of the business, meaning that our business is more, now than ever, a family establishment. Our Barbecue is prized by people throughout Trinidad, and we are renowned for having one singular location.
